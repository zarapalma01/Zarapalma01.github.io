About Me

My name is Zara Palma Reina, I am from up north and also bilingual. I am fluent in Spanish and currently learning Portuguese. I’m 24 years old and I currently live in a two-bedroom apartment here in Sarasota with my two-year-old son and husband. I’ve completed high school and used to do a fair amount of babysitting while I was in school. I have a two-year-old boy of my own who is very playful and great at sharing with other children.

He has his own bedroom which is full of interactive and learning toys for babies to toddlers to even a 7 or 8 year old. I also own a cat who tends to stay in my room. She is a tabby house kitty who I adopted from PetSmart where someone rescued her after she was hit by a car. I also have a mini poodle who is hypoallergenic so you don’t have to worry about allergies. I have a community pool, and I’m great at teaching little ones and having fun!
